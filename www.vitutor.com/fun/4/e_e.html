Ejercicio 3 resuelto

Calcular derivada de f(x) = x2 − x + 1 en x = −1, x = 0 y x = 1.

f'(−1), f'(0) y f'(1).

f'(−1) = 2(−1) − 1 = −3

f'(0) = 2(0) − 1 = −1

f'(1) = 2(1) − 1 = 1
