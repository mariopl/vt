Opera:

1 5x7y2 + 8x7y2 =

5x7y2 + 8x7y2 = (5 + 8)x7y2 = 13x7y2

2 10x3y4 − 3x3y4=

10x3y4 − 3x3y4 = (10 − 3)x3y4 = 7x3y4

3 9 · (3x2y7) =

9 · (3x2y7) = (9 · 3)x2y7 = 27x2y7

4 6x10y5z · 10x3y4z2 =

6x10y5z · 10x3y4z2 = (6 · 10)x10y5zx3y4z2 = 60 · x(10+3)y(5+4)z(1+2) = 60x13y9z3

5 30x4y3 =
6xy2

6 2x5y2z =
3x2

7 5(2x3)3 =

5(2x3)3 = 5 · (23x3·3) = 5 · (8x9) = 5 · 8x9 = 40x9

8 (−2x3)3 =

(−2x3)3 = (−2)3 x3·3 = −8x9

9 (−4x3y4)2 =

(−4x3y4)2 = 16x(3 · 2)y(4 · 2) = 16x6y8

10 (−2x4y6)3=

(−2x4y6)3= −8x(4 · 3)y(6 · 3) = −8x12y18
