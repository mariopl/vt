Matrix Inverse

The multiplication of a matrix by its inverse is equal to the identity matrix.

A · A-1  = A-1 · A = I

Properties of the Inverse Matrix

(A · B)-1  = B-1 · A-1

(A-1)-1  = A

(k · A)-1  = k-1 · A-1

(A t)-1  = (A -1)t
