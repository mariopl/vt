Logarithms

The logarithm of a number with a given base is the exponent to which it is necessary to raise the base in order to obtain the number.

a is the base, x is the number and y is the logarithm.

Calculate the Logarithm:

Common Logarithm

The common logarithm has a base of 10. It is represented by log (x).

x 0.001 0.01 0.1 1 10 100 100
y = log x −3 −2 −1 0 1 2 3

Natural Logarithm

The natural logarithm has a base of e (an irrational number with an approximate value of 2.718281828). It is represented by ln (x).
