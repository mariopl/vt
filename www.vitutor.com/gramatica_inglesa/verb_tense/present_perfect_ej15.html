Elige la opción correcta entre 'simple present', 'past simple' y 'present perfect'.

1 ____ Susan these days?




2 ___ his new film yesterday?




3 ____ a lion?




4 I ____ your mother last Sunday.




5 I haven´t got her address, but I think my husband ____ it.




6 How long ____ Mary?




7 I ____ a strange noise last night.




8 My grandmother ____ to see us every Saturday.




9 My family ____ to live here when I was a baby.




10 My father____ school at 12 and started working at that age.
