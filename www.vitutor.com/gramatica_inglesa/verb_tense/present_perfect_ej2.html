Pasado perfecto simple afirmativo II

Forma oraciones afirmativas ordenando las siguientes palabras.

1
  • all
  • exams
  • you
  • passed
  • the
  • have

You have passed all the exams.

2
  • brother
  • you
  • has
  • about
  • asked
  • Jane's

Jane's brother has asked about you.

3
  • brother
  • your
  • has
  • file
  • downloaded
  • the

Your brother has downloaded the file.

4
  • today
  • ridden
  • Susan
  • horse
  • has
  • her

Susan has ridden her horse today.

5
  • made
  • this
  • 've
  • bed
  • my
  • I
  • morning

I've made my bed this morning.

6
  • a
  • It
  • day
  • been
  • 's
  • nice

It's been a nice day.

7
  • have
  • watched
  • romantic
  • a
  • they
  • movie

They have watched a romantic movie.

8
  • the words
  • order
  • have
  • they
  • in the
  • correct
  • written

They have written the words in the correct order.

9
  • years
  • met
  • I
  • after
  • brother
  • 've
  • his
  • three

I've met his brother after three years.

10
  • concert
  • played
  • has
  • in the
  • the piano
  • Sarah

Sarah has played the piano in the concert.
