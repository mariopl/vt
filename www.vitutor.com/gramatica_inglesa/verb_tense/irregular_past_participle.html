Ejercicios

Completa las siguientes oraciones con uno de los liking verbs de la caja.

seen / eaten / lied / forgiven / grown / awoken / known / won / shone / fallen
1 Mary had and begun working long before the alarm went off.

Mary had awoken and begun working long before the alarm went off.

2 He had never a hamburguer in his life.

He had never eaten a hamburguer in his life.

3 Every ethnic background is and respected in America.

Every ethnic background is known and respected in America.

4 We've one of the guards sitting on a horse.

We've seen one of the guards sitting on a horse.

5 Only six tournaments were by players under 30.

Only six tournaments were won by players under 30.

6 Criminals are frequently for their actions.

Criminals are frequently forgiven for their actions.

7 The road was by the August moon light.

The road was shone by the August moon light.

8 He is about to tell the judge that he about acting alone.

He is about to tell the judge that he lied about acting alone.

9 The storms have left trees and thousands of people without electricity.

The storms have left fallen trees and thousands of people without electricity.

10 We have 12 acres of certified organic tomatoes for seed.

We have 12 acres of certified organic tomatoes grown for seed.
