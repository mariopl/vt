Condicional continuo

La principal diferencia con el condicional simple es que pone el énfasis en la continuidad de la acción.

Los principales usos del condicional continuo son:

 1   Para formar las oraciones condicionales hipotéticas o de tipo 2 (el verbo de la oración subordinada irá en pasado simple).

If he were here he would be cleaning

Si estuviera aquí estaría limpiando.

I would be reading if it didn´t rain.

Estaría leyendo si no lloviera.

 2   Para expresar probabilidad, posibilidad, opiniones, conjeturas.

They would be watching tv.

Estarían viendo la tv.

He would´t be living here without his parent´s help.

No estaría viviendo aquí sin la ayuda de sus padres.

 3  Para hablar de algo futuro que estaría en proceso de realización o teniendo lugar en el pasado.

I knew he would be eating.

Sabía que estaría comiendo.

He knew she would be waiting for us.

Sabía que estaría esperándonos

Formación

Afirmativo

Sujeto + should/ would + be + verbo en forma -ing

Ejemplo:

Forma larga Forma corta
I should/would be working I'd be working
You would be working You'd be working
He would be working He´d be working
She would be working She´d be working
It would be working It'd be working
We should/would be working We'd be working
You would be working You'd be working
They would be working They'd be working

Negativo

 

Forma larga Sujeto + should /would + not + be + verbo en forma -ing+
Forma corta Sujeto + shouldn´t/ wouldn´t + + be + verbo en forma -ing+
Forma larga Forma corta
I should/would not be working I shouldn´t/wouldn´t be working
You would not be working You wouldn´t be working
He would not be working He wouldn´t be working
She would not be working She wouldn´t be working
It would not be working It wouldn´t be working
We should/would not be working We shouldn´t wouldn´t be working
You would not be working You wouldn´t be working
They would not be working They wouldn´t be working

Interrogativo

Afirmativo

Should/Would + sujeto + be + verbo en forma -ing + ... ?

Negativo

Should/Would + sujeto + not + be + verbo en forma -ing + .... ?

Shouldn´t/Wouldn´t + sujeto + be + verbo en forma -ing + .... ?

Ejemplo:


Afirmativo Negativo
Should/Would I be working...?
 Should/Would I not be working...?
 Shouldn´t/Wouldn´t I be working...?
Would you be working...?
 Would you not be working...?
 Wouldn´t you be working...?
Would he be working...? Would he not be working...? Wouldn´t he be working...?
Would she be working...? Would she not be working...? Wouldn´t she be working...?
Would it be working...? Would it not be working...? Wouldn´t it be working...?
Should/Would we be working...? Should/Would we not be working...? Shouldn´t/Wouldn´t we be working...?
Would you be working...? Would you not be working...? Wouldn´t you be working...?
Would they be working...? Would they not be working...? Wouldn´t they be working...?
