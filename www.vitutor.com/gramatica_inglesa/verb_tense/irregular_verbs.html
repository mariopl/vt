Ejercicios

Elige la opción correcta entre el simple past o el past perfect.

1 My father to high school.

My father went to high school.

2 The sun suddenly in a cloudy day.

The sun arose suddenly in a cloudy day.

3 The tickets were out.

The tickets were sold out.

4 He the gardening last weekend.

He did the gardening last weekend.

5 The toaster down this morning.

The toaster broke down this morning.

6 The week before, he and Oliver had to Seattle for the Washington game.

The week before, he and Oliver had flown to Seattle for the Washington game.

7 Her bicycle was at the market.

Her bicycle was stolen at the market.

8 They beer and ate fried chiken.

They drank beer and ate fried chiken.

9 We at home all day.

We were at home all day.

10 I to buy bread.

He forgot to buy bread.
