Ejercicios del pasado simple negativo e interrogativo II

Forma oraciones interrogativas ordenando las siguientes palabras.

1
  • an
  • book
  • did
  • write
  • interesting
  • he
?

Did he write an interesting book?

2
  • Jane
  • at
  • take
  • bath
  • a
  • ten
  • did
?

Did Jane take a bath at ten?

3
  • many
  • how
  • eggs
  • buy
  • did
  • you
?

How many eggs did you buy?

4
  • in
  • travel
  • did
  • bike
  • Switzerland
  • you
  • by
?

Did you travel by bike in Switzerland?

5
  • practice
  • last
  • you
  • sport
  • any
  • did
  • year
?

Did you practice any sport last year?

6
  • did
  • how
  • go
  • bars
  • many
  • we
  • to
?

How many bars did we go to?

7
  • did
  • he
  • to
  • talk
  • when
  • her
?

When did he talk to her?

8
  • to
  • grocery
  • you
  • did
  • store
  • the
  • go
?

Did you go to the grocery store?

9
  • eat
  • did
  • last
  • you
  • pizza
  • night
?

Did you eat pizza last night?

10
  • the
  • played
  • yesterday
  • piano
  • who
?

Who played the piano yesterday?
