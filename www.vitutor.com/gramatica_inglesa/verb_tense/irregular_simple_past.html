Ejercicios

Completa las siguientes oraciones con la forma del pasado simple de los verbos en verde.

1 Mary meet her brother at the university.

Mary met her brother at the university.

2 I see him talking to his friends.

I saw him talking to his friends.

3 We be happy to visit our family.

We were happy to visit our family.

4 The plants grow in two weeks.

The plants grew in two weeks.

5 I really feel sorry to say that.

I really felt sorry to say that.

6 The teacher give a sucessful speech.

The teacher gave a successful speech.

7 You read ten books last year.

You read ten books last year.

8 He take the train at 9.30.

He took the train at 9.30.

9 The journalist write the article in ten minutes.

The journalist wrote the article in ten minutes.

10 The phone ring twice but nobody answered.

The phone rang twice but noboby answered.
