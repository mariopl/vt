Futuro simple y 'be going to'

Elige la opción correcta.

1 be a good football player?

Is your brother going to be a good football player?

2 Ann has a new friend and he to the zoo with us.

Ann has a new friend and he's going to come to the zoo with us.

3 We for a walk, if you want.

We'll go for a walk, if you want.

4 The computer is broken. a new one?

The computer is broken. Are you going to buy a new one?

5 your parent's new house?

Is that famous architect going to design your parent's new house?

6 'Would you like something to drink?' 'Thanks, I a cup of tea.'

'Would you like something to drink?' 'Thanks, I will have a cup of tea'.

7 'It's freezing cold.' 'I the heater on.'

'It's freezing cold.' 'I'll turn the heater on.'

8 a second part of this movie?

Is there going to be a second part of this movie?

9 Who the Nobel Prize this year?

Who's going to win the Nobel Prize this year?

10 When are they going to us?

When are they going to visit us?
