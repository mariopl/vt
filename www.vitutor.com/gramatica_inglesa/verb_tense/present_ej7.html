Ejercicios del presente continuo afirmativo I

Ordena las siguientes palabras para formar oraciones.

1
  • not
  • playing
  • he
  • volleyball
  • is
  • today

He is not playing volleyball today.

2
  • you
  • driving
  • fast
  • too
  • are

You are driving too fast.

3
  • the
  • guitar
  • is
  • moment
  • Mary
  • at the
  • playing

Mary is playing the guitar at the moment.

4
  • raining
  • it
  • now
  • is
  • right

It is raining right now.

5
  • watching
  • my
  • am
  • with
  • TV
  • I
  • boyfriend

I am watching TV with my boyfriend.

6
  • lunch
  • we
  • having
  • all together
  • are

We are having lunch all together.

7
  • is
  • wine
  • drinking
  • much
  • too
  • he

He is drinking too much wine.

8
  • are
  • going
  • work
  • you
  • home
  • after

You are going home after work.

9
  • studying
  • class
  • he
  • in
  • is
  • Frech

He is studying French in class.

10
  • are
  • pumpkin
  • a
  • pie
  • cooking
  • you

You are cooking a pumpkin pie.
