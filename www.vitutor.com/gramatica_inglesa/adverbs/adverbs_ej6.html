Posición de los adverbios I

Manner + place + frequency + time + purpose

Ordena las siguientes palabras para formar oraciones.

1
  • drive
  • your
  • you
  • car
  • must
  • carefully

You must drive your car carefully.

2
  • seen
  • anywhere
  • have
  • glasses
  • my
  • you
?

Have you seen my glasses anywhere?

3
  • his
  • reconized
  • hardly
  • father
  • he

He hardly recognized his father.

4
  • wears
  • jeans
  • he
  • old
  • still
  • that

He still wears that old jeans.

5
  • angrily
  • sister
  • looked
  • her
  • at
  • she

She looked angrily at her sister.

6
  • along
  • I
  • slowly
  • the
  • walked
  • street

I walked slowly along the street.

7
  • has
  • never
  • to
  • been
  • she
  • France

She has never been to France.

8
  • become
  • neighbours
  • rich
  • my
  • aren't
  • to
  • suddenly
  • going

My neighbours aren't suddenly going to become rich.

9
  • is
  • every
  • cinema lover
  • to
  • this
  • recommended
  • movie
  • highly

This movie is highly recommended to every cinema lover.

10
  • caught
  • bird
  • the
  • fast
  • the cat

The cat caught fast the bird.
