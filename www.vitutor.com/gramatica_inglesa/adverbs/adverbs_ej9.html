Otros adverbios I: only / even

Completa la oración con only o even, según corresponda.

1 He plays tennis in the rain.

He even plays tennis in the rain.

2 When she's upset, she talks to her brother.

When she's upset, she only talks to her brother.

3 She was starving! She ate a piece of dry toast.

She was starving! She even ate a piece of dry toast.

4 It's prety easy. a child could understand it.

It's pretty easy. Even a child could understand it.

5 It's the thing I can say.

It's the only thing I can say.

6 People can lie and lie under oath.

People can lie and even lie under oath.

7 He's on diet and eats pasta on Sundays.

He's on diet and only eats pasta on Sundays.

8 My sister is really active and she gets up at six on Sundays.

My sister is really active and she even gets up at six on Sundays.

9 I will do it because I love you.

I will only do it because I love you.

10 I threw my bag on the bed and sat in the chair.

I threw my bag on the bed and sat in the only chair.
