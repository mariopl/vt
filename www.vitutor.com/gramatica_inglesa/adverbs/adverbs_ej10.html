Otros adverbios II: since, for, ago

Completa la oración con since, for o ago, según corresponda.

1I haven't seen him five years.

I haven't seen him for five years.

2 I came to London, you haven't called me.

Since I came to London, you haven't called me.

3 We were at his home just five minutes .

We were at his home just five minutes ago.

4 I haven't eaten anything early morning.

I haven't eat anything since early morning.

5 I've been living in New York two years.

I've been living in New York for two years.

6 My brother usually practices the piano a couple of hours every afternoon.

My brother usually practices the piano for a couple of hours every afternoon.

7 We have been waiting for them three o'clock.

We have been waiting for them since 3 o'clock.

8 Richard finished his studies three years .

Richard finished his studies three years ago.

9 The Eiffel Tower was constructed more than one century .

The Eiffel Tower was constructed more than one century ago.

10 I haven't studied French 1997.

I haven't studied French since 1997.
