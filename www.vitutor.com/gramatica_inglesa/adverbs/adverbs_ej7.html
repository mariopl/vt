Posición de los adverbios II

Manner + place + frequency + time + purpose

Construye una oración con las siguientes palabras.

1 go / Mary / beach / and / never / Susan / to / the

Mary and Susan never go to the beach

2 father / sometimes / my / sings / shower / the / in

My father sometimes sings in the shower

3 use / at / often / school / a / computer / we

We often use a computer at school

4 at / eight / I / breakfast / half / always / past / have

I always have breakfast at half past eight

5 very / usually / is / friendly / he

He is usually very friendly

6 never / at / eat / night / they /

They never eat at night.

7 his / helps / daughter / never / he

He never helps his daughter

8 eat / vegetables / I / sometimes

I sometimeeat vegetables

9 always / at / are / me / friends / laughing / his

His friends are always laughing at me

10 friend / for / my / Tom / always / walk / goes/ a

My friend Tom always goes for a walk
