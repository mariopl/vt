Ejercicios de verbos modales XI

Elige la opción correcta.

1 I ____ go now or I'll be late.




2 Lucy ____ have been asleep because she didn't answer the phone.




3 He's a genious! He ____ write when he was three.




4 The man I was speaking to ____ be American.




5 Tell me your problem. I ____ be able to help you.




6 I suggested she ____ a new car, hers is about to break down.




7 The boss is very busy, I ____ disturb him now.




8 I was feeling sick yesterday. I ____ at all.




9 'Where are you going on holiday?' 'I ____ go to Ireland.'




10 ____ I come in, please?
