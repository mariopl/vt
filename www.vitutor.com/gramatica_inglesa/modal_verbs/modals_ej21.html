Ejercicios de verbos modales XIII

Completa las siguientes oraciones con "will", "might", "could", "won't" o "may"' + infinitivo/gerundio del verbo marcado en verde.

1 There's no point in phoning him. He (work) now and he (hear) the phone.

There's no point in phoning him. He'll (will/might) be working now and he'll not (won't/will not) hear the phone.

2 Don't forget your pullover. It (be) cold.

Don't forget your pullover. It may/might be cold.

3 (History repeat) itself in the next ten years?

May/Will history repeat itself in the next ten years?

4 What (she do) with such knowledge?

What might she do with such knowledge?

5 She (not come) . She hates parties.

She'll not (won´t/will not) come. She hates parties.

6 I'm sure Susan (wear) a spectacular dress for the wedding.

I'm sure Susan will wear a spectacular dress for the wedding.

7 It (not look) pretty, but it's dependable and very effective.

It may not look pretty, but it's dependable and very effective.

8 Every day I learn something that I (not think) is important right now, but I (use) it in the future.

Every day I learn something that I may not think is important right now, but I may use it in the future.

9 Companies (judge) on how they treat the environment.

Companies will be judged on how they treat the environment.

10 I really believe more (do) to help people in Africa.

I really believe more could be done to help people in Africa.
