Ejercicios de verbos modales: expresión de posibilidad III

A) Completa las oraciones con will/won't y might/mightn't, según corresponda.

1 I'm sure someone (buy) my bike soon.

I'm sure someone will buy my bike soon.

2 The bus (not leave) before 6 p.m.

The bus won't (will not) leave before 6 p.m.

3 I (have) enough money to buy that game.

I mightn't (might not) have enough money to buy that game.

4 We (take) the underground to arrive soon.

We might take the underground to arrive soon.

5 There (be) few tickets left for the concert.

There will be few tickets left for the concert.

6 She (need) extra lessons. Her marks are very good.

She won't (will not) need extra lessons. Her marks are very good.

7 It (rain) this afternoon, it´s very sunny at the moment.

It mightn't (might not) rain this afternoon, it's very sunny at the moment.

8 Mum (help) you later but she´s busy now.

Mum will help you later but she's busy now.

9 The teacher (explain) it again if you don't pay attention.

The teacher won't explain it again if you don't pay attention.

10 They (move) to a new house if they get some incomings.

They might move to a new house if they get some incomings.
