Ejercicios de verbos modales VI

Elige la opción correcta.

1 She have taken Lis to the café.

She shouldn't have taken Lis to the café.

2 My date with Ben be a disaster.

My date with Ben could be a disaster.

3 They talk about their problems.

They have to talk about their problems.

4 You talk on the phone during a date.

You shouldn't talk on the phone during a date.

5 I have gone by my own?

Needn't I have gone by my own?

6 I arrive on time?

Might I arrive on time?

7 We do an effort to get on well.

We should do an effort to get on well.

8 The child chat with his friends.

The child was not allowed to chat with his friends.

9 He put the meat in the oven.

He had better put the meat in the oven.

10 Fashion different in thirty years.

Fashion will be different in thirty years.
