Nombres contables e incontables: Some / any / no quantifier (---)

Elige la opción correcta.

1 I believe that people don't like drinking alcohol.

I believe that some people don't like drinking alcohol.

2 If you don't eat fruit, you can have health problems.

If you don't eat any fruit you can have health problems.

3 I'm sure that he had reasons to say that.

I'm sure that he had some reasons to say that.

4 He likes beer but wine gives him a headache.

He likes beer but wine gives him a headache.

5 I'd like milk and toasts, please.

I'd like some milk and toasts, please.

6 There weren't trees both sides of the river.

There weren't any trees both sides of the river.

7 We bought strawberry lime yogurt yesterday.

We bought some strawberry lime yogurt yesterday.

8 temperatures are rising to fifty degrees in areas of Earth.

Temperatures are rising to fifty degrees in some areas of Earth.

9 Nuclear power is taking major steps in our present civilization.

Nuclear power is taking some major steps in our present civilization.

10 I love aubergines. Are there aubergines left?

I love aubergines. Is there any aubergines left?
