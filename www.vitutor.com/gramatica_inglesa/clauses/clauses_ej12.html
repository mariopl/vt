Elige la opción correcta para expresar el estilo indirecto en pasado.

2 'Peter works in Dublin.'



3 'We are arriving in two hours.'



4 'Peter doesn't speak German very well.'



5 Paul said 'I have a flu.'



6 John: 'I don't need to buy a new bike.'



7 'Sue is coming to the party.'



8 'I'm visiting you next weekend.'



9 Tom: 'This hospital is highly specialised.'
