Ejercicios de segundo condicional

Hipotetic situations If + past simple + would/could/might + verb

A) Forma oraciones arrastrando los siguientes elementos.

You might see that movie
If it weren't rainig
I would answer you
If I bought a Porsche,
he would travel more often
If he didn't live by the coast,
if I had enough money
If he worked harder,
He might go with you
I would help people

1 I would take you for a ride.

2 I would buy an apartment

3 If Peter had more time,

4 if you go to the cinema.

5 he might earn more money.

6 he wouldn't be a sailor.

7 If I were rich,

8 If I understood you

9 if he didn't have to work.

10 we could go out.

1 If I bought a Porsche, I would take you for a ride.
2 I would buy an apartment if I had enough money.
3 If Peter had more time, he would travel more often.
4 You might see that movie if you go to the cinema.
5 If he worked harder, he might earn more money.

6 If he didn't live by the coast, he wouldn't be a sailor.
7 If I were rich, I would help people.
8 If I understood you, I would answer you.
9 He might go with you if he didn't have to work.
10 If it weren't raining, we could go out.
