Ejercicios de pasiva impersonal y pasiva reflexiva II

Reflexive passive. Completa las siguientes oraciones con los verbos en los tiempos indicados entre paréntesis.

11 My baby (be born -simple future) by the month of April.

My baby will be born by the month of April.

12 I (offer -present perfect) a job as an assistant manager.

I've (have) been offered a job as an assistant manager.

13 My sister (love) by everybody.

My sister's (is) loved by everybody.

14 John hates (invite -simple present) to many public events.

John hates being invited to many public events.

15 We (not inform -simple past) about the trip schedule.

We weren't informed about the trip schedule.

16 Nobody (guarantee -simple present) a job nowadays.

Nobody is guaranteed a job nowadays.

17 They (get divorce -simple past) and then married again after three years.

They got divorced and then married again after three years.

18 Terrorism (consider -simple past) a major threat to the security of the nation.

Terrorism is considered a major threat to the security of the nation.

19 The match (not expect -simple present) to start until eight.

The match isn't (is not) expected to start until eight.

20 They (get lost -simple past) when they went to London.

They got lost when they went to London.
