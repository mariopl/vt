Ejercicios de futuro y condicional I

A) Completa las siguientes oraciones con los verbos siguientes en la forma pasiva del futuro o condicional, según corresponda.

recognise / return / spend / cut / steal

1 The plants of the garden this afternoon.

The plants of the garden will be cut this afternoon.

2 John if he went there.

John would be recognised if he went there.

3 All our savings in my son's studies next year.

All our savings will be spent in my son's studies next year.

4 My bike (not) even if I don't lock it.

My bike won't (will not) be stolen even if I don't lock it.

5 Incomplete applications (not) to the applicant.

Incomplete applications will be returned to the applicant.

B) Convierte las siguientes oraciones a la forma pasiva.

6 They were going to sell the house yesterday.

The house was going to be sold yesterday.

7 If I knew Lucy, I would invited her boyfriend.

If I knew Lucy, her boyfriend would have been invited.
Her boyfriend would have been invited if I knew Lucy.
If I knew Lucy, her boyfriend would be invited.

8 Would she teach how to drive her students if they were eighteen?
?

Would her students be taught how to drive if they were eighteen?

9 I thought that someone was going to paint the house in two days.
I thought

I thought the house was going to be painted in two days.

10 They are going to have completed the project before the deadline.

The project is going to be completed before the deadline.
