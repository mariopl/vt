Ejercicios I - Verbo 'to have'. Presente simple

Convierte a la forma negativa las siguientes oraciones.

1 She has a shower every day.

She does not (doesn't) have a shower every day.

2 Our houses have a garden.

Our houses do not have a garden.

3 Tom has a son and a daughter.

Tom does not have (doesn't have) a son and a daughter.
Tom hasn't a son and a daughter.

4 My boyfriend has a new watch.

My boyfriend does not (doesn't) have a new watch.
My boyfriend hasn't a new watch

5 They have blue eyes.

They do not (don't) have blue eyes.
They haven't blue eyes.

6 I have enough time.

I do not (don't) have enough time.
I haven't enough time.

7 Susan has three cats.

Susan does not (doesn't) have three cats.
Susan hasn't three cats.

8Birds have four legs.

Birds do not (don't) have four legs.

9 They have an old car.

They do not (don't) have an old car.
They haven't an old car

10 Susan has an orange jacket.

Susan does not (doesn't) have an orange jacket.
Susan hasn't an orange jacket.

B) Convierte a la forma interrogativa las siguientes oraciones.

11 I have a lot of friends.
?

Do I have a lot of friends?

12 She has a new mobile phone.
?

Does she have a new mobile phone?

13 Martin hasn't a black dog.
?

Doesn't Martin have a black dog?

14 All her friends have sunglasses.
?

Do all her friends have sunglasses?

15 This hotel has rooms with high ceiling.
?

Does this hotel have rooms with high ceiling?

16 Tom and Susan have a big problem.
?

Do Tom and Susan have a big problem?

17 They haven't a good dictionary.
?

Don't they have a good dictionary?

18 He has a flat in New York.
?

Does he have a flat in New York?

19 I have black hair.
?

Do I have black hair?

20 His neighbours have Internet.
?

Do his neighbours have Internet?
