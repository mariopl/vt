Ejercicios III- Verbo 'to be'

Ordena las palabras para formar oraciones interrogativas.

1 old / how / you / are
?

How old are you?

2 friend / is / best / who / your
?

Who is your best friend?

3 are / from / you / where
?

Where are your from?

4 her / is / when / birthday
?

When is her birthday?

5 food / what / favourite / is / your
?

What is your favourite food?

6 interested / she / what's / in
?

What's she interested in?

7 so / you / why / tired / are
?

Why are your so tired?

8 Tom / where's / Susan's / party / and
?

Where's Tom and Susan's party?

9 Italy / capital /what's / the / of
?

What's the capital of Italy?

10 next / time / English / what / is / class/ our
?

What time is our next English class?
