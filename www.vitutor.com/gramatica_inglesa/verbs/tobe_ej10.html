Ejercicios IV- Verbo 'to be'

Ordena las siguientes palabras para formar oraciones.

1
  • years
  • I
  • twenty
  • old
  • am

I am twenty years old.

2
  • girlfriend
  • is
  • London
  • from
  • my

My girlfriend is from London.

3
  • car
  • your
  • is
  • old
  • not
  • very

Your car is not very old.

4
  • my
  • she
  • friend
  • wasn't
  • best

She wasn't my best friend.

5
  • sister
  • old
  • his
  • years
  • eight
  • is
?

Is his sister eight years old?

6
  • yesterday
  • weren't
  • there
  • they
?

Weren't they there yesterday?

7
  • father
  • is
  • in
  • garden
  • not
  • my
  • the

My father is not in the garden.

8
  • Manchester
  • you
  • are
  • from
?

Are you from Manchester?

9
  • not
  • building
  • same
  • they
  • the
  • are
  • in

They are not in the same building.

10
  • a
  • fan
  • am
  • music
  • pop
  • I

I am a pop music fan.
