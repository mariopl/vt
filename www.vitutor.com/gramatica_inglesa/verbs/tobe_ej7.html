Ejercicios I- Verbo 'to be'. Presente simple

A) Convierte a la forma negativa las siguientes oraciones.

1 My girlfriend is in China.

My girlfriend is not (isn't) in China.

2 Our family is here.

Our family is not (isn't) here.

3 His sisters are teachers.

His sisters are not (aren't) teachers.

4 I am from London.

I am not (I'm not) from London

5 Her name is Lucy.

Her name is not (isn't) Lucy.

6 Our grandparents are very old.

Our grandparents are not (aren't) very old.

7 My friend Peter is very smart.

My friend Peter is not (isn't) very smart.

8 I am cold.

I am not (I'm not) cold.

9 She is in the garden.

She is not (isn't) in the garden.

10 These jeans are very expensive.

These jeans are not (aren't) very expensive.
