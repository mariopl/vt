Pronombres personales sujeto, pronombres personales objeto y adjetivos posesivos

Completa la oración con me, you, him, her, it, us, you o them, según corresponda.

1 My friend Tom lives near me. I go to work with
.

him Pronombre personal objeto en la tercera persona del singular masculino (él)

2 My teachers are very good. I really like
.

them Pronombre personal objeto en la tercera persona del plural (ellos)

3 History is a difficult subject. I´m not very good at
.

it Pronombre personal objeto en la tercera persona del singular impersonal (ello)

4 Mike and I don´t listen in class. Our teacher is not happy with
.

us Pronombre personal objeto en la primera persona del plural (nosotros)

5 Rihanna is a great singer. I really like
.

her Pronombre personal objeto en la tercera persona del singular femeninio (ella)

6 Science and Geography are her favourite subjects. She's good at
.

them Pronombre personal objeto en la tercera persona del plural (ellos)

7 You´re a fantastic sister. I really love
.

you Pronombre personal objeto en la segunda persona del singular (tú)

8 I´m going to the cinema. Do you want to come with
?

me Pronombre personal objeto en la primera persona del singular (conmigo)

9 My best friend is Amy. I sit next to
.

her Pronombre personal objeto en la tercera persona del singular femenino (ella)

10 Bob and you are always laughing at me. I don´t want to play with
.

you Pronombre personal objeto en la segunda persona del plural (vosotros)
