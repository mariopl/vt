Números cardinales I

Escribe el nombre de los siguientes números cardinales.

45 = forty-five
99 = ninety-nine
18 = eighteen
485 = four hundred and eighty-five
32 = thirty-two
63 = sixty-three

108 = one hundred and eight
1524 = one thousand five hundred and twenty-four
17 = seventeen
176 = one hundred and seventy-six
211 = two hundred and eleven
781 = seven hundred and eighty-one
