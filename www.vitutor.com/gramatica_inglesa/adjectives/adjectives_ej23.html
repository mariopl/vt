Comparativos con adverbios

Completa las siguientes oraciones con las palabras de la siguiente caja.

more bored than / well enough / more important than / less entertaining than / too late / as much as / less deep than / tall enough / as soon as / so little
1 There's nothing
stopping wars.

There's nothing more important than stopping wars.

2 They arrived
for dinner at the hotel.

They arrived too late for dinner at the hotel.

3 She didn't play
to win the game.

She didn't play well enough to win the game.

4 The real frustration is that there's
that can be done.

The real frustration is that there's so little that can be done.

5 Rainfall in the country was
70 percent below normal for the year.

Rainfall in the country was as much as 70 percent below normal for the year.

6 I'm not
to play pro basketball.

I'm not tall enough to play pro basketball.

7 I promise I will arrive
I can.

I promise I will arrive as soon as I can.

8 The racing was
in previous years.

The racing was less entertaining than in previous years.

9 I felt even
before.

I felt even more bored than before.

10 The river was
I had thought.

The river was less deep than I had thought.
