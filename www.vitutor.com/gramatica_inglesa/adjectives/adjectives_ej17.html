Números ordinales y cardinales I

Escribe el nombre de los siguientes números cardinales.

1st = first
55th = fifty-fifth
20th = twentieth
32nd = thirty-second
13th = thirteenth
63rd = sixty-third

16th = sixteenth
562nd = five hundred and sixty-second
10th = tenth
1080th = one thousand and eightieth
153rd = one hundred and fifty-third
41st = forty-first
