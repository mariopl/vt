Pronombres indefinidos III

Elige la opción correcta.

1 "Your mother is at the door." "I don´t want to see "

I don't want to see anybody "any..." para oraciones negativas e interrogativas (No quiero ver a nadie)

2 They´re a very nice couple. They go together.

They go everywhere together "every..." para oraciones afirmativas (Van a todos los sitios juntos)

3 I can´t find my keys. I know they´re in this room.

I know they're somewhere in this room "some..." para oraciones afirmativas (Sé que están en algún sitio en esta habitación)

4 You look hungry. Let me give you to eat.

Let me give you something to eat "some..." para oraciones afirmativas (Déjame darte algo de comer)

5 "Do you know my brother?" "Of course, knows him."

Of course, everybody knows him "every..." para oraciones afirmativas (Todo el mundo lo conoce)

6 "Is sick?" "Yes, I feel a bit sick."

Is anybody sick? "Any..." para oraciones interrogativas (¿Está alguien mareado?)

7 I can't find my mobile phone .

I can't find my mobile phone anywhere "any..." para oraciones negativas (No encuentro mi móvil en ninguna parte)

8 Did phone?

Did anybody phone? "any..." para oraciones interrogativas (¿Ha llamado alguien?)

9 I can't understand that he says.

I can understand anything "any..." para oraciones negativas (No entiendo nada)

10 We said to each other for the entire fifteen-minute drive.

We said nothing to each other... "no..." para realizar una negación en una oraciones afirmativas (No dije nada a Peter)
