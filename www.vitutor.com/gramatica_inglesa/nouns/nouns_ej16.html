Enough / too

A) Reescribe las siguientes oraciones con las palabras entre corchetes sin cambiar el sentido de la oración.

1 He is good enough for the job. (too / good)

He is not (isn't) too good for the job.

2 My car is too small for all my family. (enough / big)

My car isn't (is not) big enough for all my family.

3 The coffee was too hot for me. (enough / warm)

The coffee was not (wasn't) warm enough for me.

4 They were speaking too fast for me to understand. (enough / slow)

They were not (weren't) speaking slow enough for me to understand.

5 The coat isn't too short. (long / enough)

The coat is long enough.
