Operations with Fractions

Priorities

1. Transform the mixed numbers and decimal numbers to a fraction:

2. Calculate the powers and roots

3. Carry out operations in the parentheses, brackets and braces.

4. Calculate the products and quotients.

5. Perform the additions and subtractions.


Example

First, work with the products and mixed numbers within the parentheses.

Operate in the first parenthesis, remove the second, simplify the third and operate in the last one.

Calculate the powers, perform the products and simplify.

Carry out the operations in the parentheses.

Complete the operations in the numerator, then divide and simplify the result.
