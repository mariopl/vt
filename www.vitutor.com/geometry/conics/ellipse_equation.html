Equation of an Ellipse

Ellipses Centered at (0, 0)

Horizontal Major Axis

F'(-c, 0)       F(c, 0)


Vertical Major Axis


F'(0, -c)      F(0, c)


Ellipses Centered at (x0, y0)

Horizontal Major Axis


By removing the denominators, an equation of the following form is obtained:

A and B have the same sign.

F'(x0−c, y0)      F(x0+c, y0)


Vertical Major Axis


F'(x0, y0−c)      F(x0, y+c)
