Equation of the Hyperbola

Hyperbolas Centered at (0, 0)

Horizontal Transverse Axis

F'(−c, 0) and F(c, 0)


Vertical Transverse Axis

F'(0, −c) and F(0, c)

Hyperbolas Centered at (x0, y0)

Horizontal Transverse Axis


F(x0+c, y0) and F'(x0− c, y0)
