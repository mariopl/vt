Orthogonal and Orthonormal Vectors

Orthogonal Vectors

Two vectors are orthogonal or perpendicular if their dot product is zero.

Example

Not perpendicular.


Orthonormal Vectors

Two vectors are orthonormal if:

1. Their dot product is zero.

2.The two vectors are unit vectors.
