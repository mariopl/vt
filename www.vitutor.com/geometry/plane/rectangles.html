Rectangle

A rectangle has equal opposite sides and 4 right angles.

The diagonals of a rectangle are equal.


Area of a Rectangle

The area of a rectangle is equal to its base multiplied by its height.

Perimeter of the rectangle

Example

Calculate the area and perimeter of a rectangle with a base of 10 cm and a height of 6 cm.


P = 2 · (10 + 6) = 32 cm

A = 10 · 6 = 60 cm2
