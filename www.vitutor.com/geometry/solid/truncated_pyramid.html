Truncated Pyramid

A truncated pyramid is the result of cutting a pyramid by a plane parallel to the base and separating the part containing the apex.

The lateral faces of a truncated pyramid are trapezoids.

The height of a truncated pyramid is the perpendicular distance between the bases.

The apothem is the height of any of its sides.
