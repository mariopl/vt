Sphere

Spherical Surface

A spherical surface is the surface generated by rotating a circle about its diameter.

Sphere

A sphere is the region of the space that is inside a spherical surface.

Center

The center is the interior point that is equidistant to all points on the surface of the sphere.

Radius

The distance from the center to any point on the surface of the sphere.

Diameter

The diameter is the distance from one point through the center to another point.
