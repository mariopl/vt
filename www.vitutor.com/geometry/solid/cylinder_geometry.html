Cylinder

A cylinder is a solid of revolution generated by a rectangle that rotates around one of its sides.

Axis

The axis is the fixed side about which the rectangle turns.

Bases

The bases are the two circles that generate the sides perpendicular to the axis.

Height

The height is the distance between the two bases.
